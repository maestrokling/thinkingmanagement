From Framework 3: Leadership Alignment Audit

Three Questions. Every Leader Answers Independently.

  1. What work stays human-only? Not "strategic thinking." Be specific. Name actual tasks, decisions, or outputs your organization has decided will not be AI-augmented. What's the principle behind that decision?
  2. What work gets AI-augmented? Humans remain in the loop, but AI assists. For what tasks, specifically? At what point in the workflow does AI enter? Who decides whether AI output is good enough to use?
  3. What work becomes AI-primary? AI handles it. Humans review outputs rather than producing them. Which tasks? What verification does the human perform? What's the escalation path when AI output is wrong?
