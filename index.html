About

Twenty years in enterprise technology taught me one thing that keeps proving itself: the organizations that win aren't the ones with the best tools. They're the ones that build the operational and cultural infrastructure to actually use them.

At Artisan Studios, I'm building the AI strategy and delivery transformation for a growing consultancy — architecting a Six-Pillar AI Adoption Framework, standing up an Innovation Lab, and designing the market shift from project execution to AI-led advisory. At AWS, I spent six years scaling professional services — growing the Southeast practice from 5 to 60 people, then leading nationally with a 900% increase in delivery capacity. As Healthcare P&L owner, I drove 100% client retention and near-perfect satisfaction scores across 20+ Fortune 500 accounts.

At Microsoft, I managed a $135M project portfolio supporting Bing Maps and geospatial engineering — delivering $60M in savings and leading a $110M aerial imagery program across the US and Western Europe. I founded Allies@ at Amazon because culture isn't a side project — it's load-bearing infrastructure.

I've been writing about the intersection of emotional intelligence and technology adoption since before 2015. The thesis hasn't changed: we keep pouring the foundation last.

Executive MBA and Master of Music — systems thinking meets creative problem-solving. I think in systems, communicate in stories, and ship on deadlines.
